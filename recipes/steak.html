Ribeye Steak

Doesn't this steak look good!

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Generously salt the steak
  2. Cook the steak in a hot cast-iron skillet until you think it's done
  3. Remove steak from skillet
  4. Add butter on top of steak
  5. Let the steak rest for 10 minutes before eating
