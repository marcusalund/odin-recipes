Margherita Pizza

Doesn't this pizza look good!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaves

Steps

  1. Apply tomato sauce on rolled out pizza dough
  2. Add chunks of mozzarella cheese
  3. Add roughly torn basil leaves
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza
  5. Cook in wood-fired pizza oven for 4-6 minutes, or until done
